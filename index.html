About MoneyLo

Empowering smarter financial decisions by democratising access to credit

MoneyLo is a financial technology company democratising access to clear, transparent, and affordable credit for millions of Indians — because we believe everyone deserves the opportunity to achieve their financial goals.

We leverage our cutting-edge technology stack to streamline the loan application process, making it easier than ever to find the right loan for your needs. Our platform offers a quick and secure way to get the best loan options from reputable banking partners approved in minutes.

Mission
  • Speed up and simplify the loan application process
  • Offer competitive rates and terms
  • Educate borrowers about loan options
  • Drive financial inclusion for new-to-credit borrowers
  • Foster financial wellness through responsible lending practices
  • Help infromal micro enterprises get registered with the government

MoneyLo is powered by Appreciate

A leading fin-tech company on a mission to democratise finance.
